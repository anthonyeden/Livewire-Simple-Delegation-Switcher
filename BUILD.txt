Simple build instructions:
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ensure you have PyInstaller installed
Ensure you have 32-bit Python 2.7 installed

Delete build/ & dist/

Run:
 - pyinstaller -p libs -i SwitchIcon.ico --version-file=version.py --onefile LW-Delegation-Switcher.py 
   (rename to LW-Delegation-Switcher-Debug.exe)
 - pyinstaller -p libs -i SwitchIcon.ico --version-file=version.py --noconsole --onefile LW-Delegation-Switcher.py

Copy config-sample.json and SwitchIcon.ico into dist/

ZIP up the dist/ folder and distribute as needed
